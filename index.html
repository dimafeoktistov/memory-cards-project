Matching Game

How to play?

Open the cards by clicking or tapping on them. You win when all 8 pairs are matched. If you finish within 12 moves you get 3 stars. If you finish after 20 moves you get 1 star.

  • S - start the game!

  • R - restart the game!
  • Space - pause/unpause the timer

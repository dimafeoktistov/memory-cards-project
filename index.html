How to play?

Open the cards by clicking or tapping on them. You win when all 8 pairs are matched. If you want to restart just press a restart button. If you want to restart just go ahead and press the restart button. If you want to Pause just hit pause button. When the game is paused you can't click any cards. If you finish within 12 moves you get 3 stars. If you finish after 20 moves you get 1 star.
